FAQ

Where is the venue?

The workshop will be held at Al Mankhool, Bur Dubai. Full details will be emailed to you upon registration.

Do I need to bring materials?

No! All pens, paper, and practice sheets are provided at the venue for free.

Is it really free?

Yes, this 2-day in-person masterclass is 100% free. Seats are strictly limited due to venue capacity.

I have bad handwriting, can I join?

Absolutely. Calligraphy is about drawing shapes, not handwriting. We start from the very basics.
